Frequently Asked Questions

Do you buy comics?

Yes! We buy single issues and entire collections. For large collections, we recommend calling ahead to schedule an appointment. We'll give you a fair, honest appraisal based on current market values.

How does the pull list work?

Simply tell us what titles you want to subscribe to, and we'll set aside a copy for you every time a new issue comes out. Your comics are bagged and boarded, and you get 10% off cover price. Pick up weekly or monthly - your choice!

Do you ship orders?

We primarily serve local collectors in-store, but we can arrange shipping for special orders and rare items. Contact us for shipping quotes on specific items.

What time do new comics arrive on Wednesday?

New comics are on the floor by 10 AM! Pull list members get early access starting at 9 AM. We recommend coming early for hot titles and variant covers.
